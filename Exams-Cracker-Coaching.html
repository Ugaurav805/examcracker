About Our Programs

What is the admission process for Exams Cracker?

The admission process involves a simple three-step procedure:

  1. Fill out the application form online or at our center
  2. Take our diagnostic assessment test to evaluate your current level
  3. Attend a counseling session where we recommend the most suitable program

The entire process can be completed within a day, and you can start classes immediately after enrollment.

What are the batch sizes for different courses?

We maintain limited batch sizes to ensure personalized attention:

  • School programs (6-12th): Maximum 25 students per batch
  • NEET & JEE programs: Maximum 30 students per batch
  • Services exam programs: Maximum 35 students per batch
  • Doubt clearing sessions: Maximum 15 students per session

Our small batch sizes ensure that each student receives adequate attention from faculty.

What is the fee structure for different courses?

Our fee structure varies based on the program and duration:

  • School programs: ₹45,000 - ₹75,000 per year
  • NEET & JEE programs: ₹85,000 - ₹1,25,000 per year
  • Services exam programs: ₹75,000 - ₹1,10,000 per year
  • Crash courses: ₹25,000 - ₹45,000 for 3 months

We offer scholarships based on merit and financial need. Installment options are also available.

How are the online classes conducted?

Our online classes are conducted through our proprietary learning platform that offers:

  • Live interactive sessions with real-time doubt solving
  • Recorded sessions for revision
  • Digital study materials and resources
  • Online tests and assessments
  • One-on-one mentoring sessions

Students need a stable internet connection and a laptop/tablet for the best learning experience.

Do you provide study materials or do students need to purchase them separately?

All study materials are included in the course fee, including:

  • Comprehensive textbooks and workbooks
  • Practice question banks
  • Previous year question papers with solutions
  • Formula sheets and quick reference guides
  • Online resources and digital content

Students do not need to purchase any additional study materials. Everything required for the course is provided by us.
